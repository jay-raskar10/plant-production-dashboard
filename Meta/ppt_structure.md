## Slide 2: The Modern Factory's "Blind Spots"
**Goal**: Identify the pain points (The Problem).
- **Manual Logging**: Paper-based reports that are outdated by the time they reach management.
- **Delayed Reactions**: Discovering a machine was down or a part was scrap 4 hours too late.
- **Quality Surprises**: Statistical shifts in quality that go unnoticed until the customer complains.
- **Data Silos**: Information trapped inside machines instead of being visible to decision-makers.

---

## Slide 3: The Nexus Solution
**Goal**: The "Big Reveal".
- **Real-Time Visibility**: A unified dashboard that shows you exactly what's happening *now*, not yesterday.
- **Proactive Quality**: SPC charts that predict failures before they happen.
- **Shop-Floor to Boardroom**: Accessible data for supervisors, operators, and plant managers.
- **Secure Integration**: Built to live inside your existing network with zero exposure to the outside world.

**Visuals**: Screenshot of the Production Dashboard UI.

## Slide 5: Quality Control (SPC): "Zero Scrap Strategy"
**Visuals**: Screenshot of the SPC Dashboard (X-Bar/R-Charts).
